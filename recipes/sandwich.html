Cheese & Onion Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Brown bread
  • Butter
  • Cheery tomatoes
  • Diced onions
  • Mayonnaise
  • Cheese
  1. Take tow slice of bread
  2. Butter one side of both slices of bread
  3. Grate some cheddar cheese and add to the bread
  4. Slice one medium tomato
  5. Add the onion to the bread
  6. Place one slice of bread on top of the filling
